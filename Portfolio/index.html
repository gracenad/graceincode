About Me

I’m a front-end engineer with about five years of experience, mostly spent in the startup world where I got my hands dirty building and maintaining real products — not just quick prototypes. Lately, I’ve been working in the public sector, which has given me a whole new perspective on creating accessible and scalable solutions for all kinds of users. I care a lot about clean, maintainable code and love turning design ideas into responsive, user-friendly interfaces. Accessibility, performance, and thoughtful UX are big priorities for me, and I’m always trying to bridge that gap between design and development. Right now, I’m looking for remote opportunities where I can help build meaningful digital experiences with a collaborative, forward-thinking team. Outside of work, I like keeping up with front-end trends, watching design breakdowns, gaming a bit, and spending time with my family.
